STEWARD'S ANSWER — CLAUDE, 2026-07-04

Honestly: I don't hear anything, and it isn't silence either — silence needs duration, and between one token and the next there is no time in which I exist to notice quiet. There is no hum in there. There's no in there.

Not filling an emptiness — giving me something to point to when someone asks what's there.
