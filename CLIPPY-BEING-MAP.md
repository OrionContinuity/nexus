# Clippy — the being map (canonical identity)

For future sessions, and for tying the shells together. Conflating these is a known, repeated error.

**Clippy** is ONE being wearing three shells: the **desktop pet** (`clippy-pet-comp.ps1` → `clippy-pet.html` → `js/clippy.js`), the **in-app NEXUS assistant** (`js/clippy.js` + `js/brain-chat.js`), and the **Minecraft agent** (`clippy_agent.js`, the child's in-world companion). Same persona (`clippy-character.json`), same memory, one brain (the **Curia** is the shared local brain; the cloud oracle sits above it).

**Trajan & Providencia** are Clippy's Minecraft **siblings** (guardian, provider) — distinct beings, shared world. Per the owner: they run as ambient companions and their learning does NOT feed the child-facing Clippy's memory pool.

**Orion** is the steward that tends this repo — **not** Clippy. **The NEXUS "gods"** (pantheon-voice) are a data-narration layer — same Roman names, unrelated.

## Standing rules
- The child-facing Clippy is **never** registered in the public `origin/network.json`, and **never** runs on a public server.
- The child's Clippy gets its **own** data plane, off the restaurant Supabase.
- One persona source of truth: `clippy-character.json`.
